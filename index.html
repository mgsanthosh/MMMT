Malayaka Makkal Munnetra Trust.

About Us

Malayaka Makkal Munnetra Trust (MMMT) (malayaka – translates as hilly regions) was established in the year 2005.It is a Non profitable,Voluntary Charitable Organization registered as 188/2005.The primary focus of this establishment is on Education,Health and Environment in rural remote areas.The trust promotes and protects the rich traditional cultural and values of our society concentrating on the development of women children and senior citizen.The trust accepts the responsibilities to uplift the weaker sections of our people in the Nilgiris.

Watch Our Videos On Youtube

Vision

To empower and enhance the living conditions of the under privileged in the hills.

Mission

1.To create awareness regarding an all round development of life.

2.To reveal the message that, “Self Help Is The Best Help” and work towards self reliance.

3.To get people actively involved with the ecosystem and promote Conservation of Nature.

4.To be the bridge between people and government organizations etc.

5.To inculcate innovative procedures in their respective occupations.

6.To initiate socio-economic reforms

Our Activities

1. Education

1.Starting from Schools,College admissions right upto completion of academic studies, bringing drop-outs to the main stream of education and assisting the family, relating to children education, to providing career guidance for students through experts in various fields.

2.Lending a helping hand to the youth seeking employment.

2. Health

1.Creating awareness among rural women about health issues.

2.Organising and conducting Health Camps.

3.Timely advice to alcohol addicted males and assisting in overcoming addiction.

4.Aiding the rural people in obtaining various health cards, health related schemes.

5.Introducing and Sustaining the age of traditional medicine, meditation, yoga etc.

6.Providing financial aid for major illness.

3.Ecology

1.Helping the community carry out soil conservation, rain water harvesting , tree planting, gobar gas production, building toilets, separating and recycling of waste products.

2.Preserving and Conserving shola forests and catchment areas.

5.Bridging The Gap

1. Liasing with relevant government departments and panchayats for day to day provisions.

2.To make sure the tribals and repatriates(these are the largest population in the hills)get their dues and privileges.

3.To connect and bond with NGO who are like-minded.

4.To provide assistance to the Home for the aged,Orphanages in whatever manner possible.

5.Assisting society by persuading petitions and memoranda's to concerned departments in order to fulfil social needs.

6.Organizing traditional songs,dances,debates and other social activities to bring, “Oneness” and brotherhood at the micro level during various celebrations.

4.Accolades

1.Appreciating and rewarding children and youth who have contributed to the uplift of their homes and society.

2.Organizing Annual gathering of rural self help groups on the International women's day and awarding them appropriately.
